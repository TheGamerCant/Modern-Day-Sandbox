Natural Resources

Natural Resources are split into three types of resource: Energy, Metals and Miscellaneous.
Energy resources require buildings to be turned into energy.

Resource Name Resource Category Notes
RESOURCE_light_crude_oil Energy Used as fuel.
RESOURCE_heavy_crude_oil Energy Requires refining into light crude oil to be used as fuel.
RESOURCE_nuclear_materials Energy Can be used for both energy and nuclear weapons.
RESOURCE_rare_earth_metals Metal Used for the production of advanced technology.
RESOURCE_steel Metal Used for basically everything
RESOURCE_special_metals Metal Used for the production of heavy equipment, e.g tanks, airplanes, etc.
RESOURCE_agricultural_produce Miscellaneous Produced by farm buildings.

State Categories

Every state has a custom state category that defines the shared building slots, maximum values for non-shared buildings (a state in Ukraine can have more Farm buildings than a state in the Sahara, for example) and any special modifiers the state may have.
